Welcome to the Community Science Club

What is CSM club

CSM Club is your gateway to a world of scientific wonders, tailor-made for young enthusiasts aged 7 to 15. Our club is a hub for inquisitive minds, dedicated to making science exciting, accessible, and unforgettable through hands-on experiments and engaging activities.

Why you should join:

1. Hands-On Learning: Explore science through cool experiments.

2. Curiosity Spark: We encourage questions and discovery.

3. Expert Guidance: Our mentors ensure safety and learning.

4. STEM Skills: Develop essential skills for future success.

5. Friendships: Make friends while working together.

6. Inspiration: Open doors to a lifelong love of science.

7. Fun and Creativity: Science is an exhilarating adventure!

Joining is easy: Just meet us at our CMS-Club lounge on a thursday between 16-18 and one of our mentors will sign you up!
